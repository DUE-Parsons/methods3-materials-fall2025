In-class Exercises

Apply cartographic concepts while looking at a map

For this exercise, you will be part of a group. Each group will work with a different cartographic concept:

  • Group 1: scale
  • Group 2: hierarchy
  • Group 3: color
  • Group 4: symbology
  1. Open the NYC Bike Map.
  2. Look at the map and come up with at least two descriptions of how your group's term applies to the map. For example, if your group has "color" one description might be, "one color choice on this map is representing the water with blue." You are describing the map, not critiquing it, so don't worry about judging the map (yet).
