In-class Exercises

Joining census data

  1. Download today's data.
  2. This part is optional. If you want to follow along with downloading data from NHGIS:
    1. Go to NHGIS and create a login if you don't have one.
    2. Click Geographic Levels and the + under Census Tract, then submit.
    3. Click Years and check 2018-2022 under 5-year ranges, then submit.
    4. Click Topics and click the + next to Tenure and Mortgage Status (under Housing > Occupancy and Tenure), then submit.
    5. Click the + next to Tenure under Select Data.
    6. Click the Continue button in the top right under Data Cart.
    7. Again, click the Continue button in the top right under Data Cart.
    8. Now you should see Review and Submit. Click Submit.
    9. You should see a table of extracts you have downloaded including the status of your current one. Once it is complete, click the Tables button under the Download Data column.
  3. In QGIS, open the data downloaded with NHGIS. If you weren't able to follow along, use the data in the folder called nhgis-occupancy-status-2022.
  4. Join the census spreadsheet to the tracts shapefile in nhgis-tracts-just-nyc. You can use the GISJOIN field for both layers.
  5. Make a choropleth showing the tracts by the percentage of housing units that are renter occupied.
